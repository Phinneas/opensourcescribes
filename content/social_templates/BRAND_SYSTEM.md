# OpenSourceScribes — Social Template Brand System

## Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary Background | Deep Navy | `#0a1628` | All slide backgrounds |
| Primary Accent | Electric Teal | `#40E0D0` | Pillar 1 (Client Whisperer) accent, borders, highlights |
| Secondary Accent | Electric Green | `#00FF41` | Pillar 3 (Educational) accent, data callouts |
| Tertiary Accent | Signal Red | `#e63946` | Pillar 2 (Exposé) accent, tension markers |
| Primary Text | White | `#FFFFFF` | Headlines, key claims |
| Secondary Text | Soft Gray | `#CCCCCC` | Body copy, supporting text |
| Surface | Dark Gray | `#1a1a2e` | Card backgrounds, inset panels |

## Typography

- **Headline font:** Bold sans-serif (DejaVu Sans Bold / Liberation Sans Bold / Arial Bold)
- **Body font:** Regular sans-serif (same family, regular weight)
- **Accent labels:** Monospace or small-caps uppercase for pillar tags
- **Hierarchy:** Headline → Sub-claim → Body → Tag/CTA

## Pillar Identity

| Pillar | Name | Color Signal | Tone | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pillar 1 | Client Whisperer | Electric Teal `#40E0D0` | Empathetic, direct, pain-first | Author pain recognition |
| Pillar 2 | Exposé | Signal Red `#e63946` | Blunt, insider, tension-forward | Industry insider info |
| Pillar 3 | Educational | Electric Green `#00FF41` | Clear, concrete, no fluff | Author website education |

## Voice Rules (from STYLE_GUIDE.md)

- Concrete over abstract — name the specific thing, not the category
- Short sentences — max 15 words
- No fluff — start with the claim, not the warm-up
- Human tone — senior peer explaining to a teammate, not a marketer
- Vary structure — no two posts should open the same way

## Banned Phrases

"Game-changer", "revolutionary", "cutting-edge", "seamless", "supercharge", "dive in",
"hidden gems", "powerful", "leverage", "utilize", "innovative", "unlock the potential",
"elevate your workflow", "in today's rapidly evolving landscape", "here's why this matters"
(unless immediately followed by something concrete)

## Platform Specs

### Instagram (Square)
- Format: 1080 × 1080 px
- Safe zone: 80px inset on all sides
- Text: max 3 lines of headline + 2–3 lines body
- CTA: bottom-right or bottom-center

### Instagram (Story / Reel Cover)
- Format: 1080 × 1920 px
- Safe zone: 120px top/bottom, 80px sides
- Text: centered vertically, left-aligned

### Threads
- Text-only (no image spec required)
- First line = the hook (strongest claim)
- Max 500 characters per post
- Thread posts: each one advances the argument
