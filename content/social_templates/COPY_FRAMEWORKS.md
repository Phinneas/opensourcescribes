# Social Copy Frameworks (Threads & Instagram)

These copy frameworks are designed to match the visual templates. They enforce the OpenSourceScribes voice rules: short sentences, concrete nouns, zero fluff, and no banned hype words.

---

## Pillar 1: Client Whisperer (Author Pain Recognition)
**Goal:** Show authors you understand exactly why their current setup is failing them, without judging them for it.
**Visual:** Teal template.

### Threads Format (Thread of 3-4 posts)
**Post 1 (The Hook):**
Most authors don't realize [specific problem with their website/setup].
They think the issue is [common misconception].
Here is what is actually happening. 👇

**Post 2 (The Reality):**
[Concrete observation about the author's situation. One sentence.]
[Second observation — what it costs them in time, money, or reader trust.]
You cannot fix this by just [common band-aid solution].

**Post 3 (The Shift):**
The fix is structural.
Instead of [bad practice], you need [specific technical or structural change].
[One sentence explaining why this works better].

**Post 4 (The CTA):**
If your site feels like it is fighting you, this is why.
[Link to resource/service] or DM me if you are stuck.

### Instagram Caption Format
Most authors don't realize [specific problem with their website/setup]. They think the issue is [common misconception].

Here is what is actually happening:

[Concrete observation about the author's situation. One sentence.]
[Second observation — what it costs them in time, money, or reader trust.]

You cannot fix this by just [common band-aid solution]. The fix is structural. Instead of [bad practice], you need [specific technical or structural change]. [One sentence explaining why this works better].

If your site feels like it is fighting you, this is why. Link in bio to fix it.

## Pillar 2: Exposé (Insider Industry Info)
**Goal:** Reveal a truth about the publishing/web industry that vendors usually hide. Create tension.
**Visual:** Red template.

### Threads Format (Thread of 3-4 posts)
**Post 1 (The Hook):**
What [industry/vendors] don't want you to know about [topic].
[The specific thing that is hidden, misrepresented, or actively obscured.] 🧵

**Post 2 (The Evidence):**
Look at the numbers.
[Specific fact or stat that proves the claim].
[Second piece of evidence — name the source or mechanism].

**Post 3 (The Impact):**
This means [the implication for the reader].
Every time you [action], you are actually [negative consequence].
It is built this way on purpose.

**Post 4 (The Alternative):**
You don't have to play that game.
[Specific open-source or alternative approach].
[Link to resource/service].

### Instagram Caption Format
What [industry/vendors] don't want you to know about [topic].

[The specific thing that is hidden, misrepresented, or actively obscured.]

Look at the evidence:
1. [Specific fact or stat that proves the claim]
2. [Second piece of evidence — name the source or mechanism]

This means [the implication for the reader]. Every time you [action], you are actually [negative consequence]. It is built this way on purpose.

You don't have to play that game. [Specific open-source or alternative approach].

Save this post so you don't forget it next time you deal with [topic].

## Pillar 3: Educational (Author Website Education)
**Goal:** Teach one specific, concrete concept about author websites clearly and without jargon.
**Visual:** Green template.

### Threads Format (Thread of 3-4 posts)
**Post 1 (The Hook):**
How [specific thing] actually works on an author website.
[One sentence: what the reader will understand or be able to do after this thread.] 🧵

**Post 2 (The Breakdown):**
Step 1: [Step or concept — concrete, named specifically].
Step 2: [Second step — what it does, not what it 'enables'].
Step 3: [Third step — the outcome or result].

**Post 3 (The Common Mistake):**
Where most people get this wrong:
They try to [common mistake].
This breaks because [technical or logical reason].

**Post 4 (The CTA):**
Keep it simple. Stick to the three steps above.
Need help setting this up? [Link to resource/service].

### Instagram Caption Format
How [specific thing] actually works on an author website.

[One sentence: what the reader will understand or be able to do after this post.]

Here is the breakdown:
1. [Step or concept — concrete, named specifically]
2. [Second step — what it does, not what it 'enables']
3. [Third step — the outcome or result]

Where most people get this wrong is trying to [common mistake]. This breaks because [technical or logical reason]. Keep it simple and stick to the basics.

Share this with another author who is struggling with their website.
