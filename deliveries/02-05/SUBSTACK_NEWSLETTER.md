### The Hidden Gem

This week, we're particularly excited about **gastown**, a workspace manager that enables the coordination of multiple Claude Code agents. By persisting work state in git-backed hooks, gastown helps developers maintain context and continuity when their agents need to be restarted, a common challenge in multi-agent workflows.

What sets gastown apart is its innovative approach to agent orchestration. Rather than losing progress when an agent is restarted, gastown ensures that the agent's work state is preserved, allowing developers to pick up where they left off and maintain a seamless development experience. This feature alone can save teams countless hours of manual effort and context switching, boosting productivity and reducing frustration.

But gastown's value extends beyond just agent management. By providing a centralized platform for managing and tracking agent-driven tasks, the tool also helps teams stay aligned on their product roadmap, ensuring that critical milestones are met and no work falls through the cracks.

If you're working with AI agents or exploring ways to streamline your software development workflows, gastown is definitely worth a closer look. Its unique capabilities and focus on developer productivity make it a standout discovery in this week's Scribe's Digest.
